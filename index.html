About Me

First things first, thanks for bearing with me while I work on making my website look good. I'm a programmer, not a designer ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

Currently I am a senior undergraduate at Texas State University. In May 2021 I will graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Mathematics (what a mouthfull). Upon completion of my degree, I intend to continue my education in pursuit of a Ph.D. in Computer Science. My graduate school applications have been submitted and I'm currently waiting to hear back.

Although I'm early in my research career, my current interests include machine learning and natural language processing. If you're interested in learning more about my ongoing research endeavors, you're in luck! I will be presenting my early reslts at the AAAI21 Undergraduate Consortium on February 02, 2021. An extended abstract of my work will also be published in the conference proceedings.

In my free time I like to read, play video games and hang out with my cat, Frank. Right now I'm reading A Tale for the Time Being by Ruth Ozeki, my first book of 2021! On my reading list this year are Mind Hunter, Where the Crawdads Sing, and The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue. If you have any book recommendations, please send them to me! You would make my day.
